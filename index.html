About Technothon 2023⚡

TECHNOTHON 2023 is the first of its kind hackathon in Mumbai. A 24- hour nonstop hackathon exclusively for the students of Polytechnic colleges pan Mumbai. Our aim is to provide a platform for students across the city to inculcate invaluable skills in product development by building projects from scratch and competing with fellow mates and in the process help identify their innovation and problem solving skills.

A platform exclusively for Polytechnic students, where students from all engineering streams can participate in a 24-hour hackathon dedicated to innovation in the universe of engineering.

7-step
process

1  Team Formation +

Forming to Storming in the contest plays a prime role to ace among your competitors.

2  Brainstorming & Problem Statement Selection +

Select a problem statement for which you can provide the best feasible solution. Additional innovation acts as brownie points.

3  Registration & Idea Submission +

Register your team on Devfolio and submit your idea in the form of ppt/pdf/video presentation through your account.

4  Shortlisting Contenders +

Candidates with the best idea get qualified for the Grand Finale for a real-time implementation.

5  The Grand Finale - 24 hours Hackathon +

Pace with your competitors in a 24hr live hackathon which involves the idea implementation through innovation.

6  Project Presentation +

Judges evaluate the overall implementation and application of the idea in terms of productiveness of the concept.

7  Winner Declaration +

Winners are awarded with prize-money worth their efforts followed by the closing ceremony.
