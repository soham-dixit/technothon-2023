About Technothon 2023‚ö°


TECHNOTHON 2023 is the first of its kind hackathon in Mumbai. A 24- hour nonstop hackathon exclusively for the students of Polytechnic colleges pan Mumbai. Our aim is to provide a platform for students across the city to inculcate invaluable skills in product development by building projects from scratch and competing with fellow mates and in the process help identify their innovation and problem solving skills.

A platform exclusively for Polytechnic students, where students from all engineering streams can participate in a 24-hour hackathon dedicated to innovation in the universe of engineering.




7-step
process

1  Team Formation +

Forming to Storming in the contest plays a prime role to ace among your competitors.

2  Brainstorming & Problem Statement Selection +

Select a problem statement for which you can provide the best feasible solution. Additional innovation acts as brownie points.

3  Registration & Idea Submission +

Register your team on Devfolio and submit your idea in the form of ppt/pdf/video presentation through your account.

4  Shortlisting Contenders +

Candidates with the best idea get qualified for the Grand Finale for a real-time implementation.

5  The Grand Finale - 24 hours Hackathon +

Pace with your competitors in a 24hr live hackathon which involves the idea implementation through innovation.

6  Project Presentation +

Judges evaluate the overall implementation and application of the idea in terms of productiveness of the concept.

7  Winner Declaration +

Winners are awarded with prize-money worth their efforts followed by the closing ceremony.

Eligibility

Eligibility


All the students pursuing a diploma in any stream of engineering are welcome to participate in Technothon 2023. They should be a student of a registered polytechnic in and around Mumbai.

Team Formation

Team Formation


All the students pursuing a diploma in any stream of engineering are welcome to participate in Technothon 2023. They should be a student of a registered polytechnic in and around Mumbai.

Idea Submission

Idea Submsission


Once your team finalizes a problem statement, you need to submit your idea in the form of an abstract and proposal while registering for Technothon 2023 on the Devfolio platform. Selection for the Grand Finale shall happen based on the proposed solution.

The format of submission shall be in any of the following: PDF/PPT/Video Presentation.

Note: Video Presentation helps the problem setters to understand your proposed solution efficiently. You can refer to the template of the PPT here: Proposal PPT Template

What next after being shortlisted?

What next after being shortlisted?


One can expect the announcement of shortlisted teams on 14th April 2023. If your team is shortlisted for The Grand Finale - a 24-hours Hackathon, you‚Äôre expected to implement the solution proposed by you. It is recommended to have at least 65% of proposed solutions, implemented and developed as a product or a prototype.

It is prohibited to have the entire product/prototype ready before the grand finale round. However, you can have the raw setup to ensure you utilize the provided 24 hours efficiently for product development.

Documents for shortlisted teams

Documents for shortlisted teams


If you're selected for the grand finale round, you are expected to provide the following documents:

1. College Identity Cards of individual team members.
2. Any Government Id Card of individual team members
3. A consent form duly filled by individual team members - would be shared with the selected team.
4. Participation confirmation letter from the college authority on college letterhead - one per team.

Winning Criteria

Winning Criteria


Every Problem Statement can have a winner, which shall be decided by the Problem Setter. There can be the following cases:

1. Winner- The best solution/product among competitors of the same Problem Statement.
2. Joint Winners - If two teams develop and present identically similar solutions/products.
3. No winners- If the problem setters find that non of the developed product solve the provided problem.

Evaluation criteria will include novelty of the idea, complexity, clarity, and details in the prescribed format, feasibility, practicability, sustainability, the scale of impact, user experience, and potential for future work progression.

Important Information
